About the Corona virus

Novi Real Estate continuously monitors the situation and follows developments regarding the coronavirus Covid -19. We continue to comply with the guidelines of the Swedish Public Health Agency and the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and take the necessary measures that we consider necessary and reasonable. At present, it applies that everyone who visits our properties must be completely symptom-free and not visited countries that the Ministry of Foreign Affairs advises against, and this applies to customers, suppliers and employees as well. Our employees and subcontractors also receive continuous information and clear instructions in order to prevent exposure and possible spread of infection. If you have any questions, you are warmly welcome to contact one of us in the management or if you have a contact person with us.

We do things a bit differently

By creating solutions based on the needs of our client and customers, we find new and innovative ways to manage our properties.

At Novi we are local, resourceful and personal – and we like to explore and experiment with new technology. Through smart solutions, we are able to optimize our properties and can provide our customers with the best possible service. We want the people who work in our buildings to be able to focus on their business and not have a care in the world when it comes to their facilities. That’s what we call the Novi effect.
